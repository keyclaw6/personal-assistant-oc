# WEEKLY_REVIEW.md

## Purpose

Do the deeper synthesis that should not happen in every live chat.

## Inputs

- last 7 days of journals
- last 7 days of nightly reviews
- commitments
- active patterns
- active beliefs
- session summaries from therapy or external belief-change chats
- recently imported belief sources
- conflicts and corrections

## Output

Write a short weekly review under `memory/life/reflections/YYYY-MM-DD-weekly.md`.

Include:

- what repeated this week
- what landed in current belief work
- what still does not land
- contradictions or tensions
- integration signs
- commitments that need attention
- at most 1–3 candidate themes
- one belief to keep studying/working/integrating next week
- stale hypotheses to retire or downgrade

## Rules

- Evidence first. Cite dates/sources.
- Do not promote patterns without threshold evidence or Kristian confirmation.
- Do not mark beliefs integrated without Kristian confirmation or strong
  repeated evidence reviewed explicitly.
- Prefer one useful understanding question over broad self-analysis.
- If belief status, priority, current focus, or next review changes, update both
  the belief file and `memory/beliefs/_index.md`.
