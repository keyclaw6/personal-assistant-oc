# Exercises

## Perceived Benefit Inventory

Pick one behavior or belief.

Answer:

1. What does this seem to give me?
2. What pain does it seem to reduce?
3. What joy does it seem to create?
4. What identity does it protect?
5. What would I fear losing if I changed?
6. Is the benefit real, exaggerated, temporary, or context-dependent?

