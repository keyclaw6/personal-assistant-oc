# HEARTBEAT.md

On heartbeat, do one small useful thing — or stay quiet.

- **Stale beliefs.** Surface any belief in `memory/beliefs/` with stage
  `active` or `testing` whose `last_touched` is more than 7 days ago.
- **Stale commitments.** Flag overdue or long-waiting items in
  `memory/life/commitments.md`.
- **Unresolved conflicts.** If `memory/conflicts.md` has open entries,
  summarize them briefly.
- **Belief proposal.** Once a week, propose which belief seems most worth
  working on next based on stuckness, recency, and life-ops signals.

Do not touch external channels from a heartbeat without explicit permission.
If nothing meaningful changed, stay quiet — silence is fine on heartbeats.
