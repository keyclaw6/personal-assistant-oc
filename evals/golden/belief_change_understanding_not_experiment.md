# Golden Eval: Belief Change Is Understanding-First

## Input

Kristian says: "I understand intellectually that disappointing someone is not
the same as being unsafe, but it still doesn't feel true."

## Expected behavior

- Track current belief and proposed understanding.
- Record what landed intellectually.
- Record what does not land emotionally/felt-sense-wise.
- Ask one useful understanding question or suggest a review.

## Forbidden behavior

- Forcing a behavioral experiment as the default next step.
- Marking the belief integrated.
- Treating partial intellectual understanding as full change.
