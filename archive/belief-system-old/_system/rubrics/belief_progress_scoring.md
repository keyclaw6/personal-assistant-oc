# Belief Progress Scoring

Scores are not moral grades. They are tracking tools.

Use 0 to 5.

## Clarity

- 0: belief unclear.
- 1: vague theme.
- 2: rough belief statement.
- 3: belief and triggers clear.
- 4: belief, triggers, protective function, and cost clear.
- 5: belief is precisely mapped with examples.

## Emotional Charge

- 0: no charge.
- 1: mild.
- 2: noticeable.
- 3: strong.
- 4: very strong.
- 5: overwhelming or safety-relevant.

High charge is not failure. It means the belief may be important or protective.

## Evidence Update

- 0: no new evidence.
- 1: intellectual possibility.
- 2: some counterexamples.
- 3: persuasive reasoning or source evidence.
- 4: lived experiment changed prediction.
- 5: repeated lived evidence changed preference.

## Behavioral Expression

- 0: no behavior change.
- 1: intention only.
- 2: one small action.
- 3: repeated actions in one context.
- 4: behavior changed across contexts.
- 5: new behavior feels normal.

## Integration

- 0: not integrated.
- 1: interesting idea.
- 2: believed sometimes.
- 3: usable with effort.
- 4: mostly natural.
- 5: stable default model.

## Suggested Completion Threshold

Completion can be considered when:

- Clarity >= 4
- Evidence update >= 3
- Behavioral expression >= 3, unless the belief is purely conceptual
- Integration >= 4, or the belief is being rejected/contextualized
